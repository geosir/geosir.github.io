George Moe's General Project Privacy Policy

December 1, 2017. Version 1.0.


Thank you for using one of my projects!

Please understand that my projects are merely personal hobby projects. They should be considered as informal, free-to-use, non-commercial services that are provided as-is with no warranty.

Data Security

I will personally work to the best of my ability to protect your data. But please do not expect industry-standard security, as there projects are funded by my own personal resources and developed in my own free time. Please do not input highly-sensitive data, such as passwords, bank account numbers, personal identification numbers, etc. into any of my projects, as I cannot gurantee that they will be transmitted or stored securely. Although I have implemented many common security protocols, anything can happen, so proceed at your own risk.

Data Collection and Storage

Regarding Page Analytics

I use Google Analytics to collect general anonymous visitor statistics on my project webpages. The types of data collected includes page- views, page events (like object clicks and views), and other forms of data collected by default by Google Analytics.

Note that Google Analytics uses cookies to help gather visitor data. If this practice concerns you, you can disable cookies or enter a private-browsing mode on your web browser. You can also discontinue use of my projects.

Regarding Service Data

Some projects recieve data that you enter as part of the service they provide (like Task data in QuestTracker, event data on WhatDo, conversation data with Ovbot projects, and account data). Understand that when you transmit data to the service, that data may be received by my server, interpreted, and stored in the course of providing the service. Key features of the service would not be possible without such collection, interpretation, and storage.

I have included informational messages where it might not be obvious that your data is being received and collected. An example of this is visible at the bottom of the Mark Ovbot page.

Use of Data

For Service

Data that is collected and/or stored by my projects will definitely be used to provide the service intended by the project. You implicitly consent to such use and the services offered by the project by using the project. (Because otherwise why would you use the project??)

For Development

I am always looking to improve my projects, so I may use data that is collected and stored by my projects to debug and improve existing projects, and develop future projects. Please understand this to be a part of the informal private nature of my projects. If you do not consent to this practice, please immediately stop using my projects.

Not for Profit

I will NEVER disclose your data to any external person or entity for any commercial reason. This means that I will not sell your data to any data- gathering corporation, interested individual, etc.

For Legal Reasons

The only case that I may disclose your data to anyone other than my- self or personnel that are primarily involved in my projects is if an official agent of the United States government has valid legal justification to obtain that data. I will work diligently and carefully in this event to only provide the data that is legally required, nothing more, nothing less.

Disclaimer

By continuing to use my projects, you agree to the terms of this privacy policy. If you do not agree, please immediately stop using by projects.
