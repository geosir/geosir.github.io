WhatDo

What will you Do today?

Club Hub goes to college! WhatDo is the next generation of Club Hub for college campuses. Focused on answering the age-old question of "What's worth doing today?", WhatDo automatically aggregates events from campus event feeds and allows students to vote on, comment, and share the events they love. Just as Club Hub seeks to streamline club communications for high schools, WhatDo seeks to package the rich abundance of events available in college into a comprehensible format.

Demonstration available by request.
