  • ClickMe | A simple button-mashing flash game[GAME][FLASH]

    Back in 6th grade, I loved to play flash games. Some of my favorite flash games included This is the Only Level, Achievement Unlocked, and Llama Adventure, all from jmbt02 of Armor Games. Inspired by his prolific and beautiful work, I wondered if I might be able to make my own. Although primitive, Click Me was my first excursion into game development, in which I learned to use ActionScript (similar to JavaScript) to compile and embed flash games on my home webserver.

    Unfortuneately, Flash quickly fell out of favor shortly after I made the first draft of the game (nowadays, I'm learning to use modern frameworks, like Unity). However, developing and embedding this game, especially with its JS-like syntax, helped introduce me to web development very early on as I entered junior high.

  • MARTHA Speaks: Implementing Theory of Mind for More Intuitive Communicative Acts[PUBLICATION][AI]

    During the summer of 2015, I worked on a research project at the University of Illinois at Chicago (UIC) Artifical Intelligence Lab. Our goal was to develop an intelligent agent embued with a "theory of mind." Theory of Mind is an important human ability to imagine what others are thinking as one observes and interacts with another. Examples include keeping track of comprehension during a conversation, understanding unspoken queues and reading into subtext, or playing a competitive game.

    The result, progammed in Java and Lisp, was MARTHA, the Mental-state Aware Real-time THinking Assistant, which, with further development, could compete with popular AI assistants like Siri and Google Now with its deep understanding of user desires. We wrote a paper describing its Theory of Mind algorithm, which we published in the proceedings of the 2015 AAAI Fall Symposia Series. Since it was accepted, I had the opportunity to travel to Arlington Virgina to present my work to the premier researchers in AI...all as a high-school sophomore!

  • QuestTracker | The To-Do List of Champions[WEBAPP][ANDROID APP]

    Life is an Adventure. Accomplish your Goals with QuestTracker!

    Ordinary to-do lists offer boring, minimalistic displays of your responsibilities. Not this app! QuestTracker believes that living and doing are interesting--so your to-do list should be interesting too!
