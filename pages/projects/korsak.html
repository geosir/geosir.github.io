Korsak Ovbot

Who says robots can't be creative?

Everyone likes robots! And everyone likes jazz! So what's not to like about RoboJazz??

Korsak Ovbot uses machine learning techniques to generate new blippy jam sessions every hour. The purpose of this is to explore the concept of "artificial creativity." I believe that some of the catchy tunes it creates from time to time shows that robots, too, can be original.

I submitted this project as the final project in a freshman seminar on Artificial and Natural Intelligence with Professor Venkatesh Murthy.
