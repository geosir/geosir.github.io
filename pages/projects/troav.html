Troav

Delivery in 5 minutes.

Troav is a startup I founded to get people the stuff they need to live their best life. We began as a physical library of things where people could cheaply and quickly borrow technology and tools for entertainment and productivity. But when the pandemic hit, we quickly discovered that today's delivery infrastructure just wasn't capable of the speed and cost needed to make our library remote. So we set out to develop from first principles a brand new way to do delivery for the 21st century.

We've successfully invented a way to use modern economies and technologies to deliver things in just 5 minutes. Now, we're working hard to expand our platform and bring to the world the completion of e-commerce.
